What we look out for

Common patterns seen in online marketplace scams, distilled into quick checks.

🧬

Duplicate posts

Near-identical wording to a listing seen recently — especially reposted within minutes — signals a scam pattern in circulation.

💸

New account + upfront payment

Accounts under two weeks old asking to be paid before handoff are weighted as high risk.

🕒

Account age

Younger accounts carry more composite-score weight, even without other red flags present.

⭐

Review history

Zero or near-zero seller ratings raise the risk score — there's no track record to trust.

⚡

Urgency & pressure

"Buy now or lose it" messaging is designed to short-circuit careful thinking. (Noted, not scored.)

🔗

External links

Requests to move the conversation to another app or site often precede a scam attempt. (Noted, not scored.)
