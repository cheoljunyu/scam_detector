What we look out for

Common patterns seen in online marketplace scams, distilled into quick checks.

💸

Off-platform payment requests

Wire transfers, gift cards, or crypto-only payments are classic pressure tactics to avoid buyer protection.

⚡

Urgency & pressure

"Buy now or lose it" messaging is designed to short-circuit careful thinking.

🏷️

Too-good-to-be-true pricing

Prices far below market value are a strong signal of a bait listing.

🌍

Shipping & location mismatches

Sellers claiming local pickup who suddenly "must ship from overseas" are a red flag.

👤

Thin or new profiles

Accounts with no history, reviews, or verification carry more risk.

🔗

External links

Requests to move the conversation to another app or site often precede a scam attempt.
